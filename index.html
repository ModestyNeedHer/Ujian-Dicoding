Genshin Impact is a 2020 action role-playing video game produced by MiHoYo/HoYoverse. The game features an anime-style open world environment and an action-based battle system using elemental magic and character-switching. A free-to-play game monetized through gacha game mechanics, Genshin Impact is updated regularly using the games as a service model; it was originally released for Android, iOS, PlayStation 4, and Windows, followed by PlayStation 5 in 2021, with an Xbox Series X/S version coming in November 2024.

About Genshin Impact

Genshin Impact is an open-world action role-playing game that takes place in the breathtaking and mysterious world of Teyvat. As a traveler from another world, you embark on a journey to reunite with your lost sibling and uncover the secrets of this land.

Key features of Genshin Impact include:

  • Vast open world to explore
  • Elemental combat system
  • Diverse cast of characters
  • Co-op multiplayer mode
  • Regular updates and events

Emilie

She is a renowned perfume designer in Fontaine. According to Calcagni, Emilie is self-employed, and the fashionable ladies of Fontaine highly seek out her products. She also works closely with detective forces and law enforcement as a "forensic cleanser" in the background, which allows her to give additional information that most people might miss at the cost of forcing her to become more familiar with death than she would like.

Navia

She is the optimistic president of the Spina di Rosula, a mantle previously held by her late father Callas. Her enthusiastic and tenacious spirit allow her to push on with helping the people of Fontaine in spite of the various losses in her life.
